Frequently Asked Questions

What is an aggregator in the music industry ?

An aggregator is a service that distributes music to various online platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music, allowing independent artists and small record labels to reach a wider audience.

Can an artist sign with a music label and use an aggregator at the same time?

Yes, an artist can sign with a music label and use an aggregator at the same time. The music label may handle some of the distribution, but an aggregator can help reach a wider audience on online platforms.

Do I need a music label or aggregator to release my music?

No, you don't necessarily need a music label or aggregator to release your music. You can self-release your music on various online platforms, but having an aggregator can help you reach a wider audience.
